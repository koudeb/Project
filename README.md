## Research Project: LinkedIn Organic Content Strategy for B2B SaaS

### Objective
To analyze how top LinkedIn creators and B2B SaaS marketers use organic content to grow audience, build authority, and drive business results.

### What I collected
- Identified 10 active LinkedIn experts who consistently publish high-quality content
- Collected their recent LinkedIn posts
- Extracted insights on content strategy, audience growth, and engagement

### Why I chose these experts
These experts were selected because:
- They actively practice what they teach (not just theory)
- They consistently publish high-performing LinkedIn content
- They focus on B2B SaaS, personal branding, and growth strategies
- Their content provides actionable insights on audience building and conversion

### Key Learnings
- Audience-first content performs better than promotional content
- Authentic storytelling and real experiences drive engagement
- Clear positioning and niche focus improve reach
- Engagement signals (comments, saves) significantly impact distribution
- Zero-click content builds trust and authority

### Project Structure
- `/research/sources.md` → List of experts with links, dates, and annotations  
- `/research/linkedin-posts/` → Posts organized by author with insights and reasoning
